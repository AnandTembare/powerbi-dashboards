Project Overview
This comprehensive Power BI dashboard provides a deep-dive analysis of Amazon's global sales performance. It is designed to help stakeholders track revenue growth, identify highly profitable product categories, and understand regional market dynamics. By transforming raw transactional data into an interactive visual story, this project highlights key drivers of e-commerce success.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Tracked

Total Sales: $119 Million

Total Profit: $33 Million

Profit Margin: 28.00%

Total Quantity Sold: 459K units

Total Orders: 53K

Key Visualizations & Business Insights

Temporal Trends: A year-over-year (YoY) bar chart (2012–2015) and a monthly profit area chart reveal seasonal spikes, indicating peak shopping periods (e.g., Q4 holidays).

Market Segmentation: A detailed pie chart breaks down sales by customer segment, showing that the 'Consumer' segment dominates at over 50%.

Geographical Reach: An interactive global map highlights sales distribution across different countries, pinpointing areas of high demand and potential expansion zones.

Product Performance: Granular bar charts categorize sales by major categories (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture) and sub-categories (Phones, Copiers, Bookcases), allowing managers to identify top-performing and underperforming inventory.

Business Value
This dashboard empowers business leaders to make data-driven decisions regarding inventory management, regional marketing spend, and seasonal promotional strategies to maximize overall profitability.
