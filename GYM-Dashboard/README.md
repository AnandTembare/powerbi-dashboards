Project Overview
This operational dashboard is tailored for fitness center management, focusing on member engagement, demographic profiling, and facility usage patterns. It provides actionable insights to improve member retention, optimize staffing schedules, and tailor marketing campaigns to the most active demographics.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Tracked

Total Visits: 8,414

Active Membership Rate: 72.63%

Average Age of Members: 36.99 years

Average Workout Duration: 9.96 mins

Cancellations: 89

Key Visualizations & Business Insights

Member Demographics: A gender split pie chart (52% Female, 48% Male) and an Age Group bar chart show that the core customer base falls within the 30-40 and 20-30 age brackets.

Retention & Subscriptions: A donut chart tracking Activity Level (73% Active vs. 27% Inactive) paired with a Subscription Type breakdown (Premium, Standard, Basic) helps identify revenue stability.

Facility Utilization: The "Attendance by Time" area chart maps foot traffic throughout the day, showing clear peaks in the morning and evening.

Monthly Footfall: A month-by-month bar chart tracks total visits, identifying peak fitness seasons (e.g., early summer/June).

Business Value
By tracking these metrics, gym managers can identify when to schedule group classes, when extra staff is needed on the floor, and which age groups to target for new membership drives, ultimately reducing churn and boosting revenue.
